NBA & WNBA Lenses

In collaboration with Snapchat’s internal teams, I created AR lenses featured across NBA and WNBA tentpole activations, including multiple All-Star events, Draft experiences, and international games.

These lenses were part of broader fan engagement programs produced by Snap teams. They were deployed across three Snap platforms, including Snapchat, Snapchat Cam through Camera Kit, and Snap AR Mirrors, reaching audiences across mobile and in-venue experiences.

Some of these experiences were also featured in Snap campaigns with Snap Stars and professional players, where lenses were amplified through creator and athlete content.
