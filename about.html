About

I’m a Santa Monica-based interaction designer and AR/XR developer creating experiences where physical and digital worlds meet.

At Snap, I contributed to AR experiences for major events and partners including the 2024 Summer Olympics, Super Bowl, Pro Bowl, NBA, WNBA, Disney, and the Los Angeles Rams. My work focuses on interactive games, stadium-scale activations, mobile AR, and AR Mirrors.

Before AR, I worked in aerospace and metal fabrication, which shaped how I approach design: buildable, scalable, and grounded in real-world constraints.

I care about interaction that feels clear, responsive, and human, whether it lives on a phone, in an AR mirror, or across a stadium screen.
